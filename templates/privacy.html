Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how Spendly handles your information.

1. Data We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide and improve our services, including:

  • Calculating spending totals and patterns

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your financial data from unauthorized access or disclosure.

4. Third Party Services

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable or financial information to other companies. We may use trusted third-party service providers to assist in operating our website, subject to their confidentiality agreements.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@spendly.example.com.
